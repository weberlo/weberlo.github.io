Bio

My name is Logan Weber, and I am a Master's student in the PLSE and SAMPL groups at UW. My research interests are at the intersection of programming languages, machine learning, and systems.

Projects

Both projects listed below are a part of the Apache TVM project.

µTVM

µTVM brings the power of TVM to bare-metal devices. By building a lightweight runtime for interacting with devices that lack operating systems, µTVM can plug directly into the TVM stack and provide automatic optimization of ML operators and easy deployment.

Relay

Relay is an intermediate representation for machine learning applications, which ditches the traditional computation-graph-based design of machine learning IRs, and instead opts to be a full-fledged programming language. The design is surprisingly similar to the language SML, with the key difference being a tensor-based type system with a lightweight form of dependency.
